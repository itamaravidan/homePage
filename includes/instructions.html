How to Play Wordle

Welcome to Wordle: Car Safety Edition! Here's how you play:

  1. Guess the Wordle in six tries.
  2. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word related to car safety. Hit the enter button to submit.
  3. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  4. Green - The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  5. Yellow - The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  6. Gray - The letter is not in the word in any spot.
  7. Use the clues to guess the word within six attempts.
Have fun, and drive safely!
